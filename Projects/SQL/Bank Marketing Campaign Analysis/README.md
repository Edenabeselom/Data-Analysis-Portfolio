# Bank Marketing Campaign Analysis

This repository contains a data analysis project focused on understanding customer behavior and the success factors of a bank marketing campaign.

## 📊 Project Overview
This analysis investigates the success rate of marketing deposits across different customer segments, specifically looking at education levels and job titles. The goal is to identify patterns that correlate with successful term deposit conversions.

## 📂 Data Summary
The analysis utilizes a dataset containing customer demographic and financial information. Key metrics calculated include:
*   **Total Customer Reach**: The volume of customers analyzed.
*   **Success Rate**: The percentage of customers who signed up for a deposit ('yes').
*   **Financial Profiles**: Insights into customer age and account balances.

## 🛠 Analysis Results
The findings are broken down into the following key categories:

### 1. Success by Education
The analysis reveals how educational background influences the likelihood of accepting a marketing offer, ranked by conversion success.

### 2. Success by Job Title
This segment explores which professional backgrounds are most responsive to the bank's marketing efforts, helping to prioritize future outreach.

### 3. General Statistics
Aggregated data provides a snapshot of the overall customer base, including average age and the range of account balances.
