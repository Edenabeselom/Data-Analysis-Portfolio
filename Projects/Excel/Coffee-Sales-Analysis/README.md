# ☕ Coffee Sales Data Analysis

## 🎯 Project Overview
This project showcases an interactive sales dashboard developed in Excel. The objective was to analyze coffee sales performance, identify top-performing customers, and visualize regional sales trends to support data-driven decision-making.

## 🛠 Key Features & Skills
*   **🧹 Data Cleaning:** Processed raw sales data for consistency and accuracy.
*   **🧮 Data Analysis:** Utilized Pivot Tables to aggregate sales by region, customer, and time period.
*   **📈 Data Visualization:** Designed an interactive dashboard featuring dynamic charts and slicers for user-friendly filtering by date, roast type, size, and loyalty status.

## 💡 Insights
*   **🌍 Market Concentration:** The analysis identified a significant concentration of sales within the United States market.
*   **⭐ Customer Loyalty:** Nearly 50% of the customer base is enrolled in the loyalty program, indicating strong potential for recurring business.
*   **🌱 Product Performance:** Identified clear demand patterns across diverse coffee types, including Arabica, Excelsa, Liberica, and Robusta.
